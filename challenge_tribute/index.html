A reflection on Steve Jobs' life:

  • 1955: Born in San Francisco to Joanne Carole Schieble and Abdulfattah Jandali and given up for adoption. Adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs.
  • 1961: Jobs family moves to Mountain View, CA.
  • 1968: Steve does an internship at Hewlett-Packard.
  • 1970: Steve meets Wozniak through a friend.
  • 1972: Graduates from Homestead High School in Cupertino and enrolls at Reed College and drops out a semester later, attending classes he wouldn't get credit for like Calligraphy.
  • 1976: Apple is co-founded with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne. They begin to sell the Apple 1 as a kit for $666.66
  • 1977: Apple is incorporated and Apple releases the Apple 2.
  • 1980: Apple goes public and Steve is now worth $200 million.
  • 1984: Macintosh becomes the first commercially successful small computer with a graphical user interface.
  • 1985: Jobs is ousted from Apple and Jobs starts NeXT Computer.
  • 1988: NeXT releases it's first computer.
  • 1993: NeXT discontinues it hardware business and is renamed to NeXT Software.
  • 1995: Jobs becomes the CEO of Pixar and Toy Story is a huge hit.
  • 1997: Jobs becomes the CEO of Apple after NeXT was acquired for $429 million
  • 1998: iMac is introduced.
  • 2001: iTunes is released.
  • 2001: The first version of Mac OS X is shipped, codenamed 'Cheetah'
  • 2001: iPod is introduced.
  • 2004: Jobs undergoes surgery for a tumor in his Pancreas.
  • 2005: iPod Nano is announced.
  • 2006: Apple's market capitalisation surpases that of Dell at the time.
  • 2011: Goes on medical leave of absence from CEO post.
  • 2011: Gives a speech at Apple's Worldwide Developers Conference, his last public appearance.
